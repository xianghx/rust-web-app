rocket_app::domain::orders

[−][src]Module rocket_app::domain::orders::model

Contains the Order and OrderLineItem entities.

The separation between Order and OrderLineItem is kind of arbitrary, and may end up being a bit of a nuisance. If this becomes the case then rather than coupling the two together even more, we should make sure they're separated.

The main idea right now is that OrderLineItem is a subset of Order for a single product. This kind of suggests it shouldn't have an id of its own, and instead should be a composite of (OrderId, ProductId). We'll probably need to come back here one day to work this out properly.
